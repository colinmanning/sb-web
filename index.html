Welcome to Setanta Berlin, the first sports club in Berlin to focus on Irish sport and cultural activities.
We run hurling and gaelic football training and practice in the South West of Berlin.​
Setanta Berlin is affiliated to the GAA under the European County Board (Gaelic Games Europe).​
