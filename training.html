TRAINING & PRACTICE

We have adult fitness and hurling training (ladies and men) every Tuesday at 19:00 at the Sochos Sportsplatz Steglitz. Weekend adult hurling is on Saturdays at 11:00, while combined Youth and Adult session on Sundays at 12:00, also at the Sochos Sportsplatz.

Keep an eye on our Facebook page for details and training news. We sometimes have a spontaneous puck about or raining session, always published on Facebook.

﻿Hurling & GAELIC FOOTBALL Training﻿ 
TUESDAY EVENING, SATURDAY & SUNDAY MORNING

Join us in Steglitz or Gleisdreieck - twice weekly for training and practice..

We have access to an all weather pitch, changing facilities via our friends at STK Hockey Club. Hurleys, sliothars (balls) are available to use, and we have gaelic football and hurling goal post for target practice.

Coaching provided for beginners and more experienced players., All are welcome, men, ladies and kids. Competitive games always at end of the mid week adult training sessions.

Afterwards we usually have a beer and chat at the STK Clubhouse or Finnegan's Irish Pub, 5 minutes walk away.

We publish event details on our Facebook page, click on the Facebook link at the top of this page.

You can get to the sportsplatz easily via U9 (Station Schloß Str), S1 (Station Rathaus Steglitz), or Bus 170.
