Setanta Berlin Gaelic Club e.V.

Welcome to Setanta Berlin, the first sports club in Berlin to focus on Irish sport and cultural activities.
We run hurling and gaelic football training and practice in the Steglitz district of Berlin.

We also encourage interest in Irish culture here in the German capital. We have started a monthly Irish music and singing session at Finnegan's Irish Pub, near Schloss Str in Steglitz.
